What To Expect When Eating in Disneyland

Dining Options

There are many dining options in magic kingdom, California Adventure, and Downtown Disney. The nicer restaurants do require reservations, and they do fill up very quickly. If you are hoping to score one, you must get your park tickets in advance and wait for the reservation to open. Right now, they are only booking two months in advance, and you have to wait for any future dates to be released.

No Reservations

If you are looking for a place without reservations, Disney offers many other options. During meal hours the lines at more popular places can get long, but if you have the Disney app there is a mobile order option that you can use. They notify you when your food is ready to pick up, and it can save you time you might have spent standing in line

Seasonal Offerings

No matter what time of year you go, there are often seasonal treats that you can enjoy all over the parks. Some of the most common places you can find these treats are: Jolly Holiday, the Mint Julep Bar, and Mainstreet USA. There are also food carts all over that have a variety of different drinks and treats.
